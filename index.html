Short Bio

I'm a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University, supervised by Prof. Mo Chen. Previously, I received a M.Eng degree from Beihang University and a B.Eng degree from Northeast University at QinhuangDao (China). My current research centers around reinforcement learning, optimal control, and their combinational use on robot learning problems. I have worked for Huawei Canada Research and Horizon Robotics to develop algorithms and tools for robot systems.
In my spare time, I like playing basketball, programming on embedded systems, and following advances in the rocket industry. I'm also a fan of detective fiction, Ellery Queen and Arthur Conan Doyle are two of my favorite writers.
